Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do we help?

    We are helping students around the world with low bandwith get their lessons from their teachers.

  • How can I access my teacher's lessons?

    Search for your teacher's name. Then, select the right teacher(they should have their school under their name). Click on them, and you should see their page, with their newest lesson. If you want to access previous lessons, click the button that says so. If your teacher does not come up after searching them, it probably means your teacher has not created an account.

  • Why should I use this website?

    This website is completely free, and allows easy access to all your teacher's lessons, allowing you to quickly study for exams, and review material you found confusing in class.
